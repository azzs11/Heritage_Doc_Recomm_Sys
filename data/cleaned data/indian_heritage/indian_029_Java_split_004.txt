Administration
Java is divided into four provinces and two special regions:
Banten, capital: Serang
Special Capital Region of Jakarta, capital: Central Jakarta (de facto)
West Java, capital: Bandung
Central Java, capital: Semarang
Special Region of Yogyakarta, capital: Yogyakarta
East Java, capital: Surabaya (which province also includes Madura Island)
(1) including the neighbouring small archipelagos of the Kangean Islands (648.55 km2), the Sapudi Islands (167.41 km2), Talango Island (50.278 km2), Masalembu (40.85 km2), the Giligenteng Islands (30.32 km2) - all the foregoing within Sumenep Regency. (2) Other offshore islands are included in this figure, but are comparatively very small in population and area; they include Nusa Barong (84.73 km2), Bawean (197.42 km2), Karimunjawa (78 km2), Nusa Kambangan (121 km2), Panaitan (170 km2), and the Thousand Islands (8.7 km2) – with a combined population of roughly 150,000 (of whom 85,320 are on Bawean, 28,809 are on the Thousand Islands, 16,200 on Nusa Barong and 10,800 on Karimunjawa).
